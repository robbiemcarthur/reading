## Paper Notes Template
When reading a paper, capture:
1. **Problem** — What problem does it solve?
2. **Key Insight** — What's the core idea?
3. **Architecture** — How is it structured?
4. **Trade-offs** — What compromises does it make?
5. **Relevance** — How does this apply to your work at SAS?
