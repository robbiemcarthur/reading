# 2026 Learning Vault

Welcome to your structured learning vault. This is organised around your 20-week roadmap for transitioning into a Tech Lead IC role at SAS Institute.

## Vault Structure

- **Learning Roadmap.md** — The master plan (root of vault)
- **01 - Books/** — Notes and takeaways from each book
- **02 - System Design/** — System design problems, solutions, and architecture notes
- **03 - Java & Spring/** — Advanced Java, Spring Boot, and framework deep-dives
- **04 - AI & Agentic/** — AI tools, MCP, agent patterns, and integration strategies
- **05 - White Papers/** — Summaries and notes on research papers
- **06 - Leadership/** — ADRs, RFCs, team strategy docs, and leadership reflections
- **07 - Weekly Journal/** — Weekly reflection entries
- **08 - Resources/** — Links, newsletters, and reference material

## How to Use This Vault

Each week, create entries in the relevant folders as you work through the roadmap. Use the Weekly Journal for Friday reflection sessions. Link liberally between notes — the graph view will reveal connections over time.
